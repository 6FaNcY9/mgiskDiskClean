Problem
-------
After Task 11 was committed, `test_imap_ingest.py::TestSourceIntegration::test_main_source_rsync_default_unchanged`
was failing with:

  FAILED tests/test_imap_ingest.py::TestSourceIntegration::test_main_source_rsync_default_unchanged
  E   argparse.ArgumentError: the following arguments are required: --timestamp

Root cause: the test called `_build_parser().parse_args(["/some/maildir", str(tmp_path)])` without
the now-required `--timestamp` argument.  The `--timestamp` flag was made required (no fallback)
as part of the determinism design, but this test was written before that requirement was enforced.

Fix
---
File: tests/test_imap_ingest.py  line 383

Before:
    args = parser.parse_args(["/some/maildir", str(tmp_path)])

After:
    args = parser.parse_args(["--timestamp", "2024-01-01T00:00:00", "/some/maildir", str(tmp_path)])
